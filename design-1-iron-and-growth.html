About Us

Local Plumbers Who Take Pride in the Work

Branch Plumbing Limited is a locally owned and operated plumbing and gasfitting company based in Vernon, BC. We serve homeowners and businesses across the North Okanagan — from Armstrong to Salmon Arm — with the same commitment to quality on every job.

We're a small team, which means you get consistent service, honest pricing, and a tradesperson who stands behind their work. No call centres, no subcontractors — just experienced plumbers who know this region and take care of our community.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes — we provide written quotes before any work begins. We'll assess the job, explain exactly what needs to be done, and give you a clear price with no hidden fees.

Are you licensed and insured in BC?

Absolutely. Branch Plumbing Limited holds all required BC trade certifications for plumbing and gasfitting, and we carry full liability insurance on every job.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Vernon, Armstrong, Enderby, Salmon Arm, Sicamous, Sorrento, and Tappen. If you're nearby and not sure, give us a call — we're happy to travel for the right job.

Do you offer emergency plumbing services?

Yes. Plumbing emergencies don't keep business hours. Call us at +1 250-241-1474 and we'll get to you as quickly as possible.

Can you work on gas lines and gasfitting?

Yes — our team includes licensed BC gasfitters. We handle new gas line installations, appliance hookups, and gas safety inspections.

How quickly can you respond to a service call?

For standard bookings, we typically schedule within 1–2 business days. For emergencies, we aim to respond the same day.
